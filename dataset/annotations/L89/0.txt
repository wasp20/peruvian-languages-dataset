Gowuka rawna gonunerune.
Shaprane koxa gowuka-gwa. 
Gi gowuka rawna
gashanigkane ga wa konigon-koxa. 
Yami gapga rawna.
Wanna wetjeta.
Wumatkaka wanna-yma.
Wushinikjetna gashanigkane ga wa 
nomatsane, ga wa shaprane, ga
wa gonunerune ga wa konigone. 
Wushinikpotutna wanna panene.
