Meeca oca tsinane omayetaquero maaroni 
ocarati icanteetaquerori jaoca ocanteari aparoni 
tsinane timatsiri ojananequite. Oaca cameetsa 
tequerani intimeni ojananequite. Impoiji ari 
itimanaque ojananequite oaca cameetsaquea 
atsotayeri ojananequite irishintsitanteari, 
impoiji osamanitanaque iotanaque oacaayetari 
caniri, coricha, machaaqui, chochoquiyetatsiri, 
arooso, avena aisati vatsatsi. Aisati aamaaco- 
ventacari cameetsa eiro imantsiatantatsi, ocaatiri 
cameetsa inquitete, impoiji aisati osataacaan- 
taqueri vacuna.
